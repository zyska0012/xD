FAQ

We have an answer to everything! Here are the most popular questions that users ask us. If you have a question that is not answered here, then write to us in one of the groups that are convenient for you.

Why does Robux not arrive for more than 120 hours?

Robux comes 120 hours exactly from the purchase of ur VIP server/gamepass. The exact date of purchase can be found only through the roblox transactions (https://www.roblox.com/transactions), in the sales of goods section (click on “summary” and select the desired section). The main thing is to calculate the arrival date correctly.

Why does my Robux not appear in roblox transactions?

Check the order status on the site and check if there is a solution to this problem, which is related to the status of ur order in the FAQ, if not, contact our support.

What is the Transfer method?

By the Transfer method, Robux issued by purchasing a gamepass in ur place, after purchasing a VIP server/gamepass R$ will processed within 120 hours. The delivery of R$ is determined by the roblox itself, it does not depend on the site.

What should i do if the site does not find the VIP-server/gamepass?

Check if this place is public, if not, then make it public. If it didnt help, then create a new place and choose this in the proccessing window on our site.

Why are my payments not going through? (Checkout problem)

Contact the support of the checkout through which u made the payment, additionaly u can request our support for help with this.

When will the site be restocked?

The Robux restocks do not occur according to schedule, suppliers replenish stocks at their discretion. Wait a while and check again.
